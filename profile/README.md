## About HaorGrix

We build intelligent systems for modern enterprises. From AI/ML pipelines to full-stack platforms to bespoke design systems, HaorGrix delivers production-grade engineering at the intersection of AI, software, and design.

-  **Enterprise AI & Data Systems** -- ML pipelines, computer vision, NLP, large-scale scraping, predictive modeling
-  **Software & Platform Engineering** -- custom SaaS, enterprise operations systems, internal tools, mobile apps
-  **Digital Experience & Growth** -- brand identity, conversion-optimized websites, marketing automation
-  **Productized Solutions** -- AI Receptionist, LMS, invoicing tools and more
-  **Engagement Models** -- end-to-end project delivery, dedicated teams, data & API licensing

## What We Do

### Enterprise AI & Data Systems
Computer vision, NLP, predictive models, anomaly detection, large-scale data ingestion, dataset curation and licensing, custom ML pipelines.

### Software & Platform Engineering
Custom SaaS platforms, enterprise operations systems, internal dashboards, customer portals, mobile apps, API integrations across the modern SaaS stack.

### Digital Experience & Growth
Brand identity systems, conversion-optimized websites and landing pages, marketing automation, lead generation engines, full digital launches.
